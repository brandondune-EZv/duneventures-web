What We Do

Three disciplines, one operating thesis.

We believe software compounds. So does intelligence. Our work concentrates on the overlap — building durable software businesses and helping other leaders build them too.

01 — Holding

Holding Company

Our portfolio consists of early-stage software ventures across personal finance, hospitality commerce, and collector markets. We own the intellectual property, operate the platforms, and invest in long-term compounding rather than quick exits.

02 — Incubation

Software Incubator

We build new software ventures in-house, applying AI-native development practices and systematic operational infrastructure from day one. Every new company inherits the compound intelligence of every prior build.

03 — Advisory

AI Advisory

We advise leaders on how to design and integrate new intelligence systems into their existing infrastructure — aligning modern AI capabilities with the operating models, processes, and teams already in place.
